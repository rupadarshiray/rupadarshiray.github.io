Let's say someone asks a question in a chat. You see a reply that is somewhere between partially and completely a word salad answer. A word salad is a bunch of words mushed together which might form a coherent sentence but definitely "makes no sense". Alright, the answer might just be partially or completely *wrong* even though it makes complete sense.

What do you do?

Is it not your obligation to correct it, rather than giving a new answer?

Of course this will not be recieved well by the replier. It'll be termed "pointless lecturing".
