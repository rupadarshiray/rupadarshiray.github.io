There was once a very good visualization tool for research papers: "research rabbit". However now it has become https://www.researchrabbit.ai/ - a slop machine.

So, I began searching for more tools like these. There is of course 

- https://www.connectedpapers.com
- https://www.litmaps.com

which are not that helpful (and is the reason of Research Rabbit's downfall?) 

Googling further led me to

- https://github.com/akashe/Paperverse
- https://github.com/FZJ-IEK3-VSA/citation-graph-builder

But these needed some installation so I looked further and found 

- https://localcitationnetwork.github.io/#contact
- https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.22159 which talks about https://hballington12.github.io/oignon/

which are so good!
